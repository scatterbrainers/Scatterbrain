“Tell me about the day I was born, Mimi!”

So I did.

“Well… when I was young, I always wanted a sister. So when you came from the hospital looking like that… I told Mom you were the wrong gender and asked her: how can we fix it. She said we couldn’t so I asked if we could send you back for an exchange ‘cause there’d been some problems with the delivery. No. Asked if I could drop you off to the orphanage myself. No. 

I guess I just got used to you over time.”
